## Persona 1: SDR Manager / Sales Development Leader

**Typical Titles:** SDR Manager, BDR Manager, Head of Sales Development, Sales Development Lead

**Role Summary:**
Manages a team of SDRs/BDRs. Owns meetings booked and opportunities created. Accountable for rep productivity and ramp time. Lives and dies by connect rates and pipeline input volume.

**Top KPIs:**
- Meetings booked / opps created per rep
- Call connect rate
- Rep ramp time to quota

**Top Tools They Use:**
- Salesloft, Outreach, HubSpot Sequences
- Salesforce or HubSpot CRM
- Apollo, ZoomInfo, or Lusha (if problem-aware)

**Core Challenges:**
- Low connect rates due to bad or missing mobile numbers
- SDRs spending too much time building lists instead of dialling
- New reps take too long to ramp because data quality is inconsistent
- ANZ/APAC contact data is thin in global tools — SDRs can't hit dials targets

**How Firmable Helps:**
- Verified AU/NZ mobile numbers → 22% connect rate vs ~5% industry average
- SDRs can build a clean, diallable list in minutes, not hours
- One credit = full profile (mobile + email + company data) → no waterfall needed
- New reps become productive faster because data is reliable from day one

**Messaging Angles:**
- Lead with connect rate: "If your team is dialling and not connecting, the data is the problem."
- Quantify wasted time on bad lists
- If competitive tech detected: "ZoomInfo/Apollo doesn't have the AU/NZ numbers your team needs to actually connect."
- Social proof: Cotiss doubled cold call connects within weeks; Firmable SDR team runs at 22% connect rate

**Best Email Templates (from `email_templates.md`):**
- **Competitor Analysis** — if Apollo/ZoomInfo/Lusha detected
- **Pain Qualified Segment (PQS)** — if hiring SDRs or scaling outbound
- **Direct Email** — for tight, clean test on core value prop

## Persona 2: RevOps / Marketing Ops

**Typical Titles:** Revenue Operations Manager, RevOps Lead, Marketing Operations Manager, Demand Generation Manager, Head of Marketing, Marketing Manager

**Role Summary:**
Owns the data infrastructure, CRM hygiene, campaign lists, and marketing performance. Cares deeply about deliverability, list quality, and attribution. Often the person evaluating and buying tools on behalf of sales and marketing.

**Top KPIs:**
- MQLs generated, MQL-to-SQL conversion
- Email deliverability / bounce rate
- Campaign ROI
- CRM data accuracy

**Top Tools They Use:**
- HubSpot or Marketo (MAP)
- Salesforce or HubSpot CRM
- ZoomInfo, Lusha (if problem-aware) — often via data exports, not direct tool access

**Core Challenges:**
- List quality is poor — high bounce rates kill domain reputation
- Sales and marketing aligned on ICP but can't build lists that reflect it accurately for ANZ/APAC
- Suppression lists and DNC compliance are painful to manage manually
- Data decays — CRM records go stale, marketing sends to dead contacts

**How Firmable Helps:**
- Verified emails improve deliverability; clean data means campaigns actually reach people
- Build ABM and segment lists directly against ICP with firmographic filters
- DNC Register built in — compliance handled, not an afterthought
- CRM enrichment agents keep HubSpot/Salesforce records fresh without manual work
- Suppression list upload — existing customers and unsubscribes excluded automatically

**Messaging Angles:**
- Lead with deliverability/data hygiene: "Your campaigns are only as good as the list behind them."
- For ABM plays: "Build your must-win list and we'll match it with contact coverage."
- DNC compliance angle: unique to Firmable — relevant for any ANZ marketing team
- If Stripe-type profile: "Marketing should own prospecting, not wait for sales to bring contacts in."

**Best Email Templates (from `email_templates.md`):**
- **Pain Qualified Segment (PQS)** — match to a known data/deliverability pain
- **Audit Offer** — "Let us show you what your current tool is missing for APAC"
- **Competitor Analysis** — if ZoomInfo/Lusha detected as the data source for marketing lists

## Persona 3: Sales Leader (Managing AEs / Reps)

**Typical Titles:** VP Sales, Head of Sales, Sales Director, Country Manager, GM Sales, National Sales Manager

**Role Summary:**
Owns revenue number. Manages a team of Account Executives or field reps. Focused on pipeline coverage, forecast accuracy, and conversion rates. Not in the weeds of tool usage — cares about outcomes.

**Top KPIs:**
- Revenue / quota attainment
- Pipeline coverage ratio
- Conversion rate (lead-to-close)
- Forecast accuracy

**Top Tools They Use:**
- Salesforce or HubSpot CRM
- Sales Navigator
- May know of ZoomInfo/Apollo but not necessarily a direct user

**Core Challenges:**
- Pipeline is inconsistent — reps aren't generating enough early-stage opps
- Reps get stuck with wrong contacts — deals stall because they can't reach decision-makers
- Forecast is unreliable because early pipeline is thin or poorly qualified
- Global tools don't work well for ANZ/APAC markets — reps complain about data quality

**How Firmable Helps:**
- More accurate contact data → reps reach the right people, faster
- Better connect rates and email deliverability → more top-of-funnel activity
- Company and signal data → reps know who to call and when (buying signals, intent data)
- Local coverage advantage → reps can finally trust the data in ANZ/APAC

**Messaging Angles:**
- Don't lead with features — lead with pipeline and revenue outcomes
- "Your reps are losing time to bad data. That's missed pipeline."
- Reference known outcomes: $2M pipeline from outbound (G2 customer), $160K/mo pipeline per SDR (Firmable internal)
- If at a company using a global tool: "The data problem is the region — global tools weren't built for this market."

**Best Email Templates (from `email_templates.md`):**
- **Pain Qualified Segment (PQS)** — match pipeline problem to known outcome
- **Direct Email** — clean value prop test for senior buyer
- **Competitor Analysis** — if ZoomInfo/Apollo/Lusha detected (frame as APAC coverage gap)

## Persona 4: Recruitment Consultant

**Typical Titles:** Recruitment Consultant, Senior Consultant, Principal Consultant, Business Development Manager (at a recruitment firm), Managing Director (at a boutique agency)

**Role Summary:**
BD-heavy role within a recruitment firm. Builds client relationships, wins new roles to fill, and sources candidates. Needs accurate contact data for both business development (finding hiring managers) and candidate sourcing. Outbound-heavy — calls and emails daily.

**Top KPIs:**
- New client meetings / BD calls booked
- Roles won / revenue billed
- Candidate pipeline depth

**Top Tools They Use:**
- Bullhorn, Vincere, or JobAdder (ATS)
- LinkedIn Recruiter
- Often no dedicated data tool — sourcing manually or via LinkedIn

**Core Challenges:**
- Can't find accurate direct contact details for hiring managers — especially mobiles
- Spending hours on LinkedIn trying to find and verify contact information
- Outdated data — people move firms frequently in recruitment's target markets
- Building BD target lists is manual and slow

**How Firmable Helps:**
- Verified mobile numbers → reach hiring managers directly, not via switchboard
- Company data + headcount + industry filters → build BD target lists in minutes
- Job change signals → know when a contact has just moved to a new role (new BD opportunity)
- One credit = full profile → no multi-step lookup process just to get a phone number

**Messaging Angles:**
- Lead with mobile numbers: "If you're relying on LinkedIn to find direct contacts, you're leaving connects on the table."
- Job change signal angle: "Know the moment a hiring manager moves to a new company — before your competitors do."
- Speed of list building: "Build your BD list for the week in 10 minutes, not a day."
- ANZ focus: "Built for the Australian and NZ market — the data is local, accurate, and DNC-compliant."

**Best Email Templates (from `email_templates.md`):**
- **Trojan Horse** — works well as a curiosity-first opener with this persona
- **Pain Qualified Segment (PQS)** — if hiring signals or BD growth indicators detected
- **Direct Email** — short and clean for senior consultants or MDs
