# Firmable GTM Engineer Context

## Company
Firmable is an Australian-founded, AI-native B2B sales intelligence platform for the APAC
region. Think ZoomInfo built specifically for Australia, New Zealand, and Southeast Asia
(SG, MY, ID, PH, HK, JP). HQ Melbourne. Backed by Airtree Ventures ($23M raised).

## What We Sell
- Proprietary database: 3M+ company profiles, 25M+ contacts, 1,000+ data attributes
- Verified local mobile numbers (22% connect rate vs ~5% industry avg)
- Buying signals: job changes, funding rounds, leadership moves, search intent
- Integrations: HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Chrome extension
- Pricing: ~$55–170/user/month. One credit = one full profile (phones + emails included)
- Key customers: Monday.com, Canon, CBRE, DHL, Robert Half, G2

## Core Value Prop
Global tools (ZoomInfo, Lusha, Cognism, Apollo) have poor APAC coverage and no local
support. We are local, accurate, compliant (AU Do Not Call Register built-in), and
significantly cheaper. For companies selling into APAC, we win on data quality and timing.

## ICP
- Companies selling B2B into Australia, NZ, and/or Southeast Asia
- Any size — startups to enterprise
- Buyers: VP/Head of Sales, Sales Ops, RevOps, SDR Managers, Founders, CFOs
- Industries: SaaS/tech, consulting, recruitment, real estate, financial services,
  professional services

## My Role
GTM Engineer. I build outbound systems and internal revenue workflows. I own:
1. Outbound at scale (Smartlead for sending, Firmable API for leads)
2. Signal-based playbooks (HubSpot tasks, CRM enrichment, rep notifications)
3. Workflow automation (n8n on server, Claude Code for orchestration)

## Outbound Channels (Active)
- Cold email only for now (primary channel)
- Output can be: email sequence in Smartlead OR task in HubSpot for rep to call
  - Call-first: high-fit accounts (e.g. detected ZoomInfo users, senior buyers)
  - Email-first: broader prospecting, lower-touch segments
- NOT doing: LinkedIn automation, video, direct mail

## Messaging Approach
Two modes depending on prospect awareness:

### 1. NOT problem-aware (smaller teams, new to outbound/BD)
- Lead with the problem they don't know they have: no data = no pipeline
- Hook: offer a free sample list of their ICP (demonstrates product immediately)
- Keep it simple — one clear CTA, short email

### 2. Problem-aware (using ZoomInfo, Lusha, Apollo, or similar)
- Lead with the APAC gap: "Your current tool wasn't built for this region"
- Technographic signal (detected ZoomInfo usage) = strong trigger for this track
- Use direct contrast: coverage, pricing, local mobiles, DNC compliance
- Proof: 22% connect rate, $2M pipeline from outbound, recognisable customer logos

## Tone for All Copy
- Problem-first, then proof, then CTA
- Confident, direct, local — not corporate or generic
- Short sentences. No fluff. No AI/SaaS clichés.
