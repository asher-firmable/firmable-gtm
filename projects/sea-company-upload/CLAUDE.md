# SEA Company Upload — Sub-Agent

## Purpose
Pre-upload HubSpot audit and company creation/update workflow for SEA company lists.

## Workflow overview

| Step | Command | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | `/sea-company-upload-check` | Audit each domain against HubSpot; save viable lists |
| 2 | `/sea-company-viable-not-in-hubspot` | Create new HubSpot records for viable companies not in HubSpot |
| 3 | `/sea-company-viable-already-in-hubspot` | Update Company Owner (SEA) + outreach status for viable companies already in HubSpot |
| 4 | — | After steps 2 & 3, Claude generates the combined "Viable Companies" CSV |

## What goes in
- Drop one CSV into `input/` before running `/sea-company-upload-check`
- Required: at least one domain column — accepts `domain`, `website`, `company_website`, `company_domain`
- Optional but recommended: `Firmable ID`, `APAC Sales HC` (used for ICP mapping when creating new companies)

## What goes out

**From `/sea-company-upload-check`:**
- Terminal summary (3 sections: presence, outreach status breakdown, total viable)
- `output/viable_not_in_hubspot.csv` — viable companies not in HubSpot
- `output/viable_in_hubspot.csv` — viable companies already in HubSpot

**From `/sea-company-viable-not-in-hubspot`:**
- `output/viable_companies_new.csv` — newly created companies with their status

**From `/sea-company-viable-already-in-hubspot`:**
- `output/viable_companies_existing.csv` — updated companies with their status

**Final (generated by Claude after both commands):**
- `output/Viable Companies.csv` — combined, with columns: Company Name, Domain/Website, Firmable Company ID, Exists in HubSpot, Outreach Engagement Status

## Scripts

| Script | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `scripts/check_company_hubspot_status.py` | Shared — audit CSV against HubSpot, save viable lists |
| `projects/sea-company-upload/scripts/list_owners.py` | List HubSpot users (for owner selection) |
| `projects/sea-company-upload/scripts/list_status_options.py` | List outreach engagement status options |
| `projects/sea-company-upload/scripts/create_new_companies.py` | Create new HubSpot companies for viable-not-in-HubSpot list |
| `projects/sea-company-upload/scripts/update_existing_companies.py` | Update Company Owner (SEA) and status for viable-in-HubSpot list |

## Conventions
- Drop one CSV into `input/` at a time; the check script picks the most recently modified one
- `output/` is gitignored — never commit output files
- Required env var: `HUBSPOT_ACCESS_TOKEN` in `.env`
- Run from repo root with `PYTHONPATH=.`

## ICP Match (SEA) tiers (for new company creation)
| APAC Sales HC | ICP Match (SEA) | HubSpot API value |
|---|---|---|
| 0–4 | SMB | `true` |
| 5–9 | Medium | `false` |
| 10–24 | High | `High` |
| 25+ | Very High | `Very High` |
