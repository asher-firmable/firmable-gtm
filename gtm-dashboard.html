Key Challenges
Enrichment Layer Overhead
Every list requires a full enrichment pass before sending. After extracting from Snowflake, companies must be validated through Clay, pushed back into Firmable, then downloaded before outreach begins. Copywriting per vertical adds further lead time — the ramp-up phase is the hardest part.
Q3 Capacity Pressure
Running industry campaigns in parallel with a heavy Q3 events calendar creates real bandwidth risk. Conference prep, exhibitor outreach, and event follow-up all compete for the same resources — this needs to be sequenced carefully to avoid dropping either workstream.
