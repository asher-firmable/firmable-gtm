Recruitment
~11 companies matched · Dominant vertical in dataset · JobAdder integration is a primary acquisition lever
Goal

Fill more roles and win more client companies. Revenue is per placement or retainer — so pipeline volume and speed directly equal income. Every day a role goes unfilled or a client goes uncontacted is money left on the table.

  • → Win new client companies with open roles (BD motion)
  • → Reach hiring managers directly before roles go to market on Seek or LinkedIn
  • → Source niche candidates (allied health, legal, aged care) that generic databases don't carry
  • → Automate the "who is hiring right now?" research that currently eats hours each day

  • No direct contact info. LinkedIn Recruiter gives profiles, not mobile numbers. Every outreach is a connection request that may not be seen for days — or ever.
    A.I.R Recruitment, HUBB SYDNEY, Blue Bike Solutions
  • Same contacts they already know. Apollo and Lusha scrape LinkedIn, so their AU data overlaps entirely with what recruiters already have from years on the platform.
    Nodal Talent (3-week-old startup evaluated Apollo vs Firmable vs Lusher)
  • No niche vertical depth. Apollo has no ANZ healthcare vertical. Generic tools don't carry allied health professionals, legal sector contacts, or aged care role-level data.
    ACM Healthcare (radiologists/nurses), GraceWell Group (allied health), KPA Search (lawyers)

    Chain Reaction, Vectura Recruitment, Jobwire
  • ATS friction. CSV export → clean → import into JobAdder kills hours on every prospecting run.
    Elias Recruitment, Vectura Recruitment, ACM Healthcare
